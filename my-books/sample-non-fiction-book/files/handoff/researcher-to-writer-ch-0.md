## Key Findings

1. **High Failure Rate**: 80-95% of retail crypto traders lose money, with 90% of new traders failing in the first year
2. **Emotional Trading**: Fear and greed drive most trading decisions in volatile 24/7 markets, leading to systematic losses
3. **Leverage and Fees**: Most significant losses come from overleveraging and underestimating cumulative trading fees
4. **Survivorship Bias**: Media coverage focuses on rare success stories, creating false perception of easy profits
