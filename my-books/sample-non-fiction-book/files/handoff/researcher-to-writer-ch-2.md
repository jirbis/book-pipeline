## Key Findings

1. **Trading Fees Revenue**: Top exchanges earn $3-5B annually from fees alone (0.1-0.5% per trade)
2. **Spread vs. Commission**: Some platforms use 1-3% spreads instead of explicit fees, costing more
3. **Funding Rates**: Perpetual contracts charge 0.01-0.1% every 8 hours, compounding to ~45% annually
4. **Liquidation Profits**: Exchanges profit from liquidation fees during volatile periods ($10B in May 2021)
5. **Volume Paradox**: Exchanges want MORE trading, not better trading (more fees)
