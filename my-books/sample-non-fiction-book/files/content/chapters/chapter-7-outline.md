# Chapter 7 — Bitcoin: The Market's Gravity Center

## Goal
Ground readers in why Bitcoin sets the tone for the entire market and how to trade with that gravity in mind.

## Thesis
Understanding Bitcoin’s role as digital gold and market anchor prevents you from misreading altcoin cycles and keeps your portfolio from drifting into high-risk bets.

## Section Outline
### Opening — "Bitcoin is boring" (and why that’s wrong)
- Challenge the misconception that BTC is just old tech.
- Show how BTC dominance quietly shapes every alt move.

### Section 1 — Bitcoin as store of value
- Digital gold narrative; institutional adoption milestones.
- Why stability and liquidity matter to traders.

### Section 2 — BTC dominance and market tides
- How dominance shifts precede or follow alt seasons.
- Reading dominance charts to time rotations.

### Section 3 — Halving cycles and volatility rhythms
- Brief history of halving events and post-halving behaviors.
- Implications for position sizing and holding periods.

### Section 4 — Role of Bitcoin in your stack
- Using BTC as base collateral vs. trade asset.
- When to stay in BTC and skip altcoin risk.

### Closing and transition
- Summarize Bitcoin as the reference frame.
- Lead into Ethereum/altcoins chapter by contrasting value vs. utility narratives.

## Examples and Stories
- Missing an alt season because you ignored BTC dominance.
- BTC holdings softening portfolio drawdowns during bear markets.

## Data/Research Targets
- BTC price history and halving timelines.
- Dominance chart snapshots around major cycles.
- Institutional adoption data points.

## Drafting Notes
- Keep focus on practical trading implications, not ideological debates.
- Use charts/metrics readers can replicate.
