## Intake Checklist (fill before work starts)
- **Audience:** Who is this for? (age, interests, expertise level)
- **Tone & Voice:** Desired tone (formal/conversational/academic/literary) and any must-keep voice traits.
- **Research Needs:** Key topics that require research or fact-checking; critical sources to consult or avoid.
- **Constraints:** Deadlines, word-count limits, sensitivity/ethics guidelines, IP or licensing constraints.
- **Success Criteria:** How will we judge a chapter/book is “done”? (quality bar, review steps, approval owners)
